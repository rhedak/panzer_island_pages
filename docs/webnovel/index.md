# Panzer Island: The Novel

<div class="webnovel-cover">
  <img src="../assets/webnovel/0_title.webp" alt="Panzer Island novel cover showing Katyusha, Erika, Maria, and Nadeshiko on a clifftop overlooking the island coastline">

Something went wrong on Panzer Island. The population was evacuated.
The research facility went dark. The AI defense units stood down and did not start again.

Erika wakes on the island with no memory of any of it. Her credentials still work.
Her body knows how to activate the units, run the panels, navigate the facility, before
her conscious mind catches up. She does not know why.

She reactivates three AI units: Katyusha, who gives status reports on things she cannot
account for; Nadeshiko, who reaches for memories and finds gaps; and Maria, who calls
Erika "Doc" like she has said it many times before.

Someone is leaving messages in red paint across the island. The messages suggest that
whatever happened was not an accident, and that the answers are at the center.

Erika designed the system at the heart of this. She does not remember what she did with it.

*Panzer Island* is a post-catastrophe mystery told in alternating chapters: Erika's
present-day investigation and flashback chapters that reveal what the island looked like
before. The truth builds one layer at a time, and each layer reframes the last.

New chapters release weekly on Saturdays (approx. 10pm JST). The novel is free.
