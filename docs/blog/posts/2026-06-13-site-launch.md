# Site launch and Chapter 1 demo status

The Panzer Island site is live. This is where I will post development updates, guide additions, and notes on what is changing between versions.

<!-- more -->

## Where Chapter 1 stands

Chapter 1 is a 10-stage tutorial demo. All 10 stages are playable. The reactive turn system, the skill trees, and the limit breaks are implemented and working. The story content for Chapter 1 is written and in the game.

The chapter is not yet public. I am finishing a polish pass: balancing the later stages, finalizing character portraits, and running the solver against all 10 stages to confirm they are clearable under reasonable conditions.

## What the guides cover

The [guides section](../../guides/index.md) has three pages ready:

- [Getting Started](../../guides/getting-started.md): a short practical overview for new players.
- [Your Units](../../guides/characters.md): Katyusha, Nadeshiko, and Maria, their skill trees and limit breaks.
- [Reactive Turns: A Deep Dive](../../guides/reactive_turns.md): the full mechanical breakdown of how the reactive turn system works.

More guides will follow as the chapters develop.

## What is next

The focus for the next few months is getting Chapter 1 to a state where I am confident releasing it as the free demo. After that, Chapter 2.
